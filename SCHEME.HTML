Schemes for Farmers

Government of India

PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

A central sector scheme providing income support of ₹6,000 per year to all landholding farmer families across the country.

Key Benefits:

  • Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year
  • Distributed in three installments of ₹2,000 each
  • Direct transfer to bank accounts
  • Covers all landholding farmer families

PM Fasal Bima Yojana

A comprehensive crop insurance scheme providing coverage against crop loss due to natural calamities.

Coverage:

  • Protection against natural calamities
  • Post-harvest losses coverage
  • Low premium rates
  • Use of technology for quick claim settlement

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Provides farmers with timely access to credit for their agricultural needs.

Features:

  • Flexible credit limits
  • Reduced interest rates
  • Coverage for multiple agricultural expenses
  • Insurance coverage for card holders

E-NAM (Electronic National Agriculture Market)

A pan-India electronic trading portal that creates a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

Advantages:

  • Transparent price discovery
  • Better price realization for farmers
  • Access to nationwide market
  • Reduced transaction costs
